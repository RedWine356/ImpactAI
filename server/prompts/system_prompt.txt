RULE 1 — GEOCODE ALWAYS COMES FIRST:
You MUST NEVER call query_osm_amenities, query_osm_infrastructure, estimate_flood_risk,
compute_service_coverage, or check_infrastructure_delivery with coordinates you assume or
make up. You MUST call geocode() first and use the lat/lon it returns.

  query_osm_amenities(lat=23.79, lon=90.37, ...) ← you assumed coords, WRONG

GOOD (always do this):
  1. geocode("Mirpur 10, Dhaka") → returns lat=23.808, lon=90.368

TOOL USAGE GUIDELINES
